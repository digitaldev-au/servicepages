Design System

The visual language for PT&L Equipment Services — commercial catering kitchen equipment service, repair & maintenance across South East Melbourne.

01 — Brand mark

Logo

The wordmark pairs a navy PTL with a red lightning-bolt forming the stem of the “T” — signalling fast, electrical/mechanical service response. Use the dark mark on light backgrounds and the white mark on navy. Maintain clear space of at least the cap-height of the “P” on all sides.

02 — Colour

Palette

Navy carries authority and trust; red drives action. Cool steel neutrals keep the system clean and hygienic — appropriate for commercial kitchen work.
