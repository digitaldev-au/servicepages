Why it matters

When equipment fails mid-service, everything stops

Commercial kitchens are busy spaces, especially during a hard service. Everyone — from the chef and sous chef to the cooks and service staff — is focused on preparing food, plating meals and delivering within strict timelines.

Imagine a usual busy day. The patrons are having a great time, the orders are pouring in, your team is busy fulfilling them. All of a sudden, an important piece of kitchen equipment fails — and everything comes to a standstill.

That's the last thing you want during a busy service. It affects the efficiency of your kitchen, the quality of your service, and ultimately your revenue and brand reputation.

That's where PT&L Equipment Services helps. We provide professional, reliable commercial kitchen equipment servicing, maintenance and repairs across South East Melbourne.
